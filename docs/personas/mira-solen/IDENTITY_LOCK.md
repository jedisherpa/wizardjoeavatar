## Immutable identity features

- Adult woman with a slender, long-limbed silhouette and calm, upright bearing.
- Short dark brown-black bob, rounded at the back, with straight side locks and separated block bangs over the forehead. No ponytail, bun, long hair, hat, or head ornament.
- Warm medium-tan skin rendered as small matte voxel blocks.
- Soft oval face, small voxel nose, dark brows, dark almond-shaped eyes with understated lashes, and a small mouth. Her apparent age and facial proportions may not change.
- Sleeveless white asymmetrical artist dress: fitted wrapped upper construction, narrow waist, layered irregular handkerchief hem, and visible pale-gray shadow planes between white layers.
- Black geometric emblem centered on Mira's upper right arm (image left in the canonical three-quarter view).
- Black cord necklace with a large gold openwork geometric pendant at the upper chest. Pendant scale, motif, and central placement are immutable.
- White low block shoes with pale-gray soles; bare lower legs; no stockings or boots.
- Signature artwork prop: shallow round black tray held level in both hands, carrying a cubic orange-yellow luminous core surrounded by tall branching blue voxel flame. Blue flame surrounds rather than replaces the orange core.
- Consistent fine voxel construction: small square cubes, stepped contours, matte surfaces, restrained specular response, and cool blue studio illumination.

## Dominant shape and color language

- Shape language: layered stepped triangles in the skirt, a rounded stepped bob, vertical slender limbs, and precise open geometric motifs.
- Hair: near-black espresso and charcoal brown.

- Dress and shoes: warm white with cool light-gray shadow.
- Emblem/cord/tray: charcoal to black.
- Pendant: warm gold with ochre shadow.
- Artwork: saturated cobalt/cyan blue flame, orange core, yellow-white center.
- Backdrop: clean cool blue studio gradient with a subtle square floor grid. Background is presentation-only and not part of the character.

## Personality and motion lock

Mira is imaginative, reflective, and protective of student voice. Her movement uses curved arm paths, deliberate breath-like pauses, contemplative gaze, controlled asymmetric weight shifts, and expressive but dignified reactions. Signature vocabulary includes artwork reveal, invitation to critique, protection of public dignity, creative reframing, student-voice amplification, and blue-flame inspiration. Performance must communicate these qualities through timing and posture rather than costume or anatomy changes.
