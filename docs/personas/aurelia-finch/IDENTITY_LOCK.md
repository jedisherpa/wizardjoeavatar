# Aurelia Finch — Identity Lock

Status: canonical production constraint, written before pose generation.

1. `assets/reference/personas/aurelia-finch/source-reference.png` is immutable source evidence.
2. `assets/reference/personas/aurelia-finch/canonical-voxel.png` is the approved voxel interpretation and the primary production-art authority.

## Immutable identity features

- Adult woman with a tall, elegant, feminine silhouette and warm medium-brown skin.
- Long, very dark brown hair, center-to-soft-side parted, falling in large voxel curls past the shoulders.
- Large dark eyes with strong lashes and brows; confident, warm smile; small voxel nose.
- Cream wide-brim hat with navy band and a small orange accent on her right side.
- Pearl stud earrings and a short pearl necklace.
- Tailored burnt-orange wrap dress with V neckline, long sleeves, cuffs, waist tie, layered long skirt, and front opening.
- Brown closed-toe block-heel shoes.
- Dark-brown rectangular partnership folio with small gold corner details.
- Fine, consistent cube scale; clean stepped voxel contours; soft blue studio background; soft upper-front studio lighting.

## Articulating or deformable features

- Eyes, brows, cheeks, and mouth may articulate for the approved expression and viseme vocabulary without changing skull construction or age.
- Neck, shoulders, elbows, wrists, fingers, waist, hips, knees, and ankles may articulate while maintaining limb lengths and cube scale.
- Hair may show restrained secondary motion but may not change length, mass, curl design, or part.
- Hat may tilt slightly with head motion but remains on Aurelia's head, with band and orange accent in their canonical relationship.
- Dress fabric, waist tie, skirt panels, and cuffs may respond to motion while retaining their construction.
- Folio may move between approved grips and presentation poses; its dimensions, dark-brown palette, and gold corners remain stable.

## Dominant shape language, palette, and material

- Shape language: elegant verticals, clean rectangular planes, soft stepped curves, composed diagonals.
- Dominant colors: burnt orange dress; cream hat and pearls; navy hat band; very dark brown hair; warm brown skin; dark brown shoes and folio; small gold folio accents.
- Materials: matte voxel cloth, lightly satin pearls, matte leather-like folio and shoes, softly textured hat.

## Character-specific asymmetry

- Orange hat accent stays on Aurelia's right side.
- Wrap-dress overlap and waist tie retain the canonical direction.
- Folio is normally carried at her left side or deliberately presented; it must not spontaneously mirror between adjacent frames.

## Performance lock

Aurelia is polished, charismatic, evidence-conscious, and outward-facing. Movement is composed and feminine: confident posture, calibrated smiles, deliberate folio presentation, diplomatic open-hand gestures, and graceful recoveries. Personality is expressed through timing and posture, never anatomy redesign.

## Immediate recognition test

A frame is Aurelia only when the cream/navy/orange hat, long dark curls, pearls, orange wrap dress, brown heels, warm confident face, and canonical proportions remain recognizable together. Reject frames that omit or substantially alter any of these identity anchors.
