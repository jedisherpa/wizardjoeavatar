## Known limits

- Generated source sheets are not orthographic engineering drawings. Each module
  requires independent bounds and anchor review.
- White floor steps and soft contact shadows must be classified deliberately during
  isolation; they must not be removed merely because they are light-colored.
- Camera-board Joe imagery is non-canonical and must never be traced.
- The camera boards may contain pose or crop imperfections. They authorize shot intent
  only, not character pixels.
- Blank display interiors remain editorial insertion zones and must not be filled by
  the character engine.

## Approval boundary

This pass approves the v2 visual direction and preserves its source evidence. It does
not admit any v2 graph to runtime. Graph admission is a separate one-module-at-a-time
process defined in `PIXEL_GRAPH_ADMISSION.md`.
