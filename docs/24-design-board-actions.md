# Implement the Design-Board Actions

Create these actions:

```text
idle
speaking
explaining
walking
thinking
pointing
magic_cast
reaction
```

## Idle

- slight body breathing
- occasional blink
- minor hat movement
- staff remains stable
- no constant large motion

## Speaking

- mouth animation
- mild hand movement
- eye contact toward viewer
- expression supplied separately

## Explaining

- open-hand gesture
- elbow extends
- palm points upward
- staff hand stays stable

## Thinking

- free hand toward beard or chin
- eyebrows lower slightly
- gaze moves up and to the side
- body becomes mostly still

## Pointing

- free arm extends
- hand points toward a screen position
- eyes follow the point target

## Magic cast

- staff raises
- cyan orb brightens
- cyan `*`, `+`, and `.` effect cells appear
- effect expands and fades procedurally
- no full-screen flashing

## Reaction

- small body recoil
- eyebrows raise
- hands open slightly
- return to the previous stable state
