## Decision

**V1 is not fit to advance.** The planted stance, scheduled blinks, exact
frame ownership, and basic eye direction are credible. The 90-degree turn and
subsequent profile hold still require another animation pass.

## Timestamped Findings

- **Major, 7.75-7.92 seconds:** Eye lead lasts about two frames, then the head
  snaps from front to full profile in one frame. At quarter speed
  (31.00-31.67 seconds) this reads as a clear pop rather than head follow.
- **Major, 7.92-8.13 seconds:** The blink masks the turn but cannot hide the
  abrupt silhouette and neck/collar seam. The settle has no visible overshoot
  or easing.
- **Major, 8.13-12.00 seconds:** The extended profile hold feels frozen. The
  9.08-9.21 second blink briefly interrupts it but does not restore listening
  presence.
- **Moderate, 0.00-7.50 seconds:** The stance remains planted and stable and
  the scheduled blinks read, but eye changes are mechanical and the body
  contributes little responsive listening behavior.

## Required Correction

1. Add visually distinct intermediate head-follow silhouettes rather than a
   one-frame front/profile substitution.
2. Make settle motion visible at normal speed with restrained easing or
   overshoot that preserves the planted body.
3. Add subtle profile listening modulation without moving the root, feet,
   staff, or support contact.
